Blackjack Rules

You win:

  • By drawing a hand value that is higher than the dealer's hand value
  • By the dealer drawing a hand value that goes over 21.
  • By drawing a hand value of 21 on your first two cards, when the dealer does not.

Dealer wins:

  • Your hand value exceeds 21.
  • The dealers hand has a greater value than yours at the end of the round

Playing:

To start, the player and the dealer will have 2 cards. However you can only see one of the dealers cards. The player must then
decide whether they want another card (HIT Button) or if they would like to remain with the cards they have (STAND Button).
The reset button will restart the game.
